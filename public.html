Public Relations Internship

Title

Public Relations Internship

Description

Assisting in PR campaigns, drafting press releases, media monitoring, managing social media accounts, and organizing events or press conferences.

Duration

4 months (September-December)

Skills Required

Excellent written and verbal communication skills, creativity, familiarity with social media platforms, and ability to multitask.

Compensation

Unpaid; may offer stipend or course credit.

Remote or Onsite

Flexible; may include a combination of remote work and onsite events or meetings.

Organization Information

PR agency, marketing communications firm, or in-house PR department of a company.

Application Process

Submit a resume, cover letter, and writing samples showcasing communication skills and creativity.

Portfolio Showcase

Include examples of press releases, social media content, or event planning projects.

Communication Channels

Email, phone, or video conferencing for remote interns; in-person meetings and team collaboration tools for onsite interns.

Feedback and Evaluation

Regular feedback sessions with PR supervisors and opportunities to participate in PR campaigns and projects.
