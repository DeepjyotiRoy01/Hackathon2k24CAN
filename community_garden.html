Community Garden Volunteer

Title

Community Garden Volunteer

Description

Assisting in garden maintenance tasks such as planting, weeding, watering, and harvesting crops, maintaining garden infrastructure, and organizing community events or workshops.

Duration

Flexible; volunteers can choose shifts based on availability.

Skills Required

Interest in gardening and sustainable agriculture, ability to perform physical tasks outdoors, teamwork, and willingness to learn.

Compensation

Unpaid; volunteers contribute their time and effort to support community gardening initiatives.

Remote or Onsite

Onsite; requires presence at the community garden or urban farm.

Organization Information

Local community garden, urban farm, or nonprofit organization promoting sustainable food systems and community engagement.

Application Process

Fill out a volunteer application form available on the organization's website or in-person at the community garden.

Portfolio Showcase

Not applicable for volunteering roles; however, previous experience with gardening or community involvement may be mentioned in the application.

Communication Channels

Direct communication with garden coordinators via email, phone, or in-person meetings.

Feedback and Rating System

Informal feedback may be provided by garden coordinators during shifts; volunteers may also participate in periodic feedback sessions to discuss their experiences and suggestions for improvement.
