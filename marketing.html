Title

Marketing and Social Media Internship

Assisting in the development and implementation of marketing strategies, managing social media accounts, creating content, and analyzing performance metrics.

3 months (May-August)

Strong written and verbal communication skills, familiarity with social media platforms, basic understanding of marketing principles, and ability to work in a team.

Flexible; some tasks may be performed remotely, while others require onsite presence.

Marketing agency or company specializing in digital marketing and brand promotion.

Application Process

Submit a resume, cover letter, and portfolio (if available) showcasing relevant skills and experiences.

Portfolio Showcase

Include examples of social media posts, marketing campaigns, or content creation projects.

Communication Channels

Email, phone, or video conferencing for remote interns; in-person meetings for onsite interns.

Feedback and Evaluation

Regular feedback sessions with supervisors to review performance and provide guidance for improvement.
